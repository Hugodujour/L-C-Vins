Our Story

Defender of French Terroirs on the International Stage.

Matthieu and Jean Compeyrot are two brothers from winemaking families in Pomerol and Sauternes.

After experiences in wine production and trade abroad, they decided to create L&C Wines in 2015.

Driven by their attachment to the French terroir and the people who shape it, Matthieu and Jean work to share their stories in the domestic and international market.

Export

Present on all 5 continents, we offer our expertise and knowledge of the French vineyards to provide you with the most suitable range of wines for your market.

Our strengths: a suitable warehouse, a wide variety of wines, a logistics and administrative team that meets your needs, and a responsive sales team.

French Restauration

In close collaboration with our clients, we contribute to the creation of wine lists for over 300 French restaurants.

Our strengths include extensive knowledge of the French vineyards, a perfectly suited warehouse with over 200 references, and flexible and fast delivery. We ensure delivery within 3 business days.

Branding & Support

Our extensive knowledge of the French vineyards allows us to source wines for clients who wish to develop their own brand.

We also take care of label creation, bottle procurement, corks, and packaging boxes, providing you with a turnkey solution.

Sourcing

As Bordeaux wine merchants, we have a close relationship with all the Grand Crus Classés of Bordeaux. Through "The Place", we purchase our wines en primeur and for immediate delivery.

Outside of Bordeaux, our buyer traverses the rest of the French vineyards to discover the future great producers.
